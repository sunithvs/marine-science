Department of Marine Science

School of Marine Science, a unique institution of its kind in the country was established in 1938 as Department of Marine Biology and Fisheries under the erstwhile Travancore University to cater Marine Science education and research at national levels. The school is presently part of Cochin University of Science and Technology.
